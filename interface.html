High-speed (Wirelink) Interface - General

The trains provide station information, which includes eight variables for every station:

0Start offset of platform 1
1End offset of platform 1
2Track ID of platform 1
3(not used)
4Start offset of platform 2
5End offset of platform 2
6Track ID of platform 2
7(not used)

These eight variables are located at offset 57344 + (lineID-1)*800 + stationID*8, where for example for station 216 (line 2, station #16) the offset would be 57344 + 1*800 + 16*8 = 58272

There is also information about current schedule. All times are in seconds. Each entry in the schedule is:

0Arrival station
1Arrival platform
2Departure time
3Arrival time

All entries are located at offset 49152+64 + entryID*4. All times in the schedules are in seconds, starting from midnight.
